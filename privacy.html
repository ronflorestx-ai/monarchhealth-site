Last updated: September 04, 2025

Monarch Health ("Monarch," "we," "us") operates the website at monarchhealth.ai. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. How we use information

  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers who help us host and operate the website or email, and when required by law.

Data retention

Emails you send us are retained as long as needed to address your inquiry and maintain reasonable business records. Server logs are retained per the policies of our service providers.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards appropriate to the nature of the information we process. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children’s privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us to request deletion.

Information on this website is for general informational purposes only and is not medical advice. In an emergency, call 911 or your local emergency number.
